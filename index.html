About PRO 1 GLOBAL HOME CENTER

The biggest building materials and home living product shopping center in Myanmar

PRO1 GLOBAL HOME CENTER has an experience of home building and living store center 10 years, which offers construction material, home decorative products, tools and equipment used in construction, home improvement, and household and gardening decoration in modern trade warehouse style. Currently, PRO 1 Global Home Center has totally 11 branches and trying to expansion branches to cover all Myanmar region and become the leader of one stop service home living product center.

CSR Activities

Donated to Hlaing Tharyar General Hospital

On 19 July 2020, The PRO 1 Global Home Center has donated the necessary medicine and air conditioner to Hlaing Tharyar General Hospital for the second time.

Donated to rescue worker

On 19 July 2020, The PRO 1 Global Home Center was donated lunch, cold drink, and water to 200 persons while they are doing rescue work near the Shwege Reservoir for the flooding of the Ayeyarwaddy River in Amarapura Township, Mandalay.

Blood Donation Ceremony

On 6 AUG 2020, The PRO 1 Global Home Center Families was doing community blood donation at all branches for the second time.

Donated 100 PPE uniforms to the Mon State Government

On 5 Oct 2020, The PRO 1 Global Home Center has donated 100 PPE uniforms to the Mon State Government for use in Covid 19 disease control and treatment activities.

Donated to Covid 19 Control and Emergency Response Committee in East Dagon Township

On 17 Sep 2020, The PRO 1 Global Home Center has donated 2,000,000 Kyats (two hundred thousand kyats) and purified drinking water to the Covid 19 Control and Emergency Response Committee in East Dagon Township to be used for the health needs of the people.

Donated 3 Protective Chambers to Mandalay Region Government

On 19 October 2020, The PRO 1 Global Home Center has donated 3 Protective Chambers to Mandalay Region Government for use in checking Covid 19 disease at Quarantine Center.
